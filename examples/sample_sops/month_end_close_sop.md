# Month-end Close Workflow Note

Fictional company: Cedar Grove Retail Ltd

## Trigger

Month-end close begins on working day one after the period end.

## Owner

Financial Controller owns close calendar governance and final review.

## Systems

Microsoft Dynamics 365 Finance; close checklist spreadsheet; shared reconciliation evidence folder.

## Steps

- Open the close checklist and confirm task ownership.
- Post recurring and manual journals.
- Prepare balance sheet reconciliations.
- Review overdue tasks and unresolved reconciliation items.
- Complete controller sign-off and archive evidence.

## Controls

- Journal approval by journal type and account risk.
- Reconciliation preparer and reviewer sign-off.
- Evidence attachment before close completion.

## Reports

Close status dashboard, overdue task report, journal approval exception report, and reconciliation ageing.

## Pain Points

Late close tasks; manual close status tracking; missing reconciliation evidence.

## Audit Evidence

Journal approval, reconciliation workbook, reviewer sign-off, close checklist timestamp, and exception notes.
