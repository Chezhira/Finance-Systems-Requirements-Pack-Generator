# Accounts Payable Workflow Note

Fictional company: Northstar Trading Ltd

## Trigger

Supplier invoice is received in the shared AP mailbox or through the supplier portal.

## Owner

AP Process Owner is responsible for workflow design, exception review, and month-end sign-off.

## Systems

Odoo Finance purchase ledger; shared AP tracker; supplier mailbox; payment approval workflow.

## Steps

- Receive invoice and confirm supplier record.
- Match invoice to purchase order and goods receipt where applicable.
- Route invoice to the correct approver based on amount and cost centre.
- Hold invoices with missing approval, duplicate warning, or supplier master issue.
- Prepare approved invoices for payment batch review.

## Controls

- Duplicate invoice review.
- Approval threshold check.
- Supplier bank detail review.
- Three-way match exception review.

## Reports

Aged approval backlog, blocked invoice list, and payment readiness summary.

## Pain Points

Manual approval chasing; duplicate invoice risk; weak supplier master control evidence.

## Audit Evidence

Invoice image, approver, approval timestamp, exception reason, and payment batch reference.
