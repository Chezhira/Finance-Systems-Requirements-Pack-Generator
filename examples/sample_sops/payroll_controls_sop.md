# Payroll Controls Workflow Note

Fictional company: Riverstone Care Ltd

## Trigger

Payroll cycle starts after HR submits starter, leaver, salary change, and absence inputs.

## Owner

Payroll Controls Owner coordinates payroll change review, reconciliation, and finance sign-off.

## Systems

Sage Payroll, Xero Finance, HR change forms, payroll bureau portal, and bank approval workflow.

## Steps

- Receive HR payroll change inputs before cutoff.
- Validate starter, leaver, and salary change approvals.
- Review payroll register against HR input summary.
- Post payroll journal to finance system.
- Approve payment file and retain sign-off evidence.

## Controls

- Starter and leaver approval check.
- Payroll change approval before processing.
- Payroll register to GL reconciliation.
- Payment file approval evidence.

## Reports

Payroll variance report, payroll-to-GL reconciliation, payment file approval report, and exception log.

## Pain Points

Starter and leaver control gaps; payroll change approval delays; manual payroll input checks.

## Audit Evidence

Approved HR forms, payroll register, GL journal reference, bank approval evidence, and reviewer sign-off.
